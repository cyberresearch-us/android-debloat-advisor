Family Link and parental controls

Uninstalling apps with ADB does not replace parental controls. Use Google Family Link (or your platform’s equivalent) together with any debloat plan.

Official Google Family Link

  • Family Link — get started
  • Google Families help center
  • Set up Family Link

Checklist

  1. Child Google account supervised in your family group.
  2. Family Link on your phone and the child’s device.
  3. Require approval for new Play installs where possible.
  4. Turn on web / content filters (options vary by region).
  5. Review Install unknown apps per-app permissions on the child device.

Samsung

Check Samsung support for your model: parental controls, Samsung Kids, or similar — names change with One UI.
