What to expect

The AI stops at safety gates. Nothing destructive happens until you say so.

  1. Connection check — AI confirms the phone is connected. You verify the model.
  2. Package scan — AI reports how many apps it found. You say continue.
  3. Sort and review — AI shows a Remove / Keep / Review table. You pick what gets removed.
  4. Dry run — AI shows every command it will run, without running them. You say "execute".
  5. Results — AI shows success/failure for each package.
  6. Test — You test the phone. If anything broke, the AI helps restore it immediately.

After that, the AI reminds you to turn off USB debugging and disconnect. The whole process takes about 10 minutes.
